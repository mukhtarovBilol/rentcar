Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT CONDITIONS MUST I FOLLOW WHEN RENTING A CAR?

When renting a car, the minimum age requirement is 24 years, and the driving experience with a driver's license must exceed 2 years.

CAN FOREIGN CITIZENS RENT A CAR?

If you are a foreign citizen, when renting a car, you need to have your passport and driver's license with you. Additionally, according to the legislation of the Republic of Azerbaijan, foreign citizens can drive a car with a foreign driver's license for up to 1 month after entering the country. If the entry period exceeds 1 month, this entails a fine for the driver and confiscation of the vehicle. For other rules, the conditions specified in points 1 and 2 apply.

IS A DEPOSIT REQUIRED?

Depending on the rental period and the car, it is necessary. The minimum deposit starts from 100 manat.

WHAT DOCUMENTS ARE REQUIRED FOR RENTAL?

If you are a citizen of the Republic of Azerbaijan, an identity card and driver's license are sufficient.
