CAR RENTAL TERMS IN BAKU

The car rental terms in Baku of our company make the car rental process even easier than you could imagine!

RENTAL PERIOD:

  • Car rental in our company is for a minimum of 3 days.
  • The rental period starts from the moment of signing the vehicle handover/acceptance document.

PAYMENT TERMS:

  • 100% payment of the rental period is made at the time of receiving the car by the client.
  • A security deposit of 300 manats is required, which is refunded to the client in case of no breakdowns and accidents.

    • Payment is accepted:

  • In cash and by credit card (VISA / MASTERCARD)

MAIN TERMS:

  • The minimum age of the vehicle rental user must be at least 24 years old.
  • The vehicle rental user must provide a passport or international ID card and an international driver's license.
  • The driving experience of the vehicle rental user must be at least 1 year.

USE OF THE CAR:

  • There is no limit on the daily mileage of the car.
  • The car can only be used within the territory of the Republic of Azerbaijan.

CAR RENTAL RATE IN BAKU INCLUDES:

  • Insurance of cars against theft, accidents, and third-party damage
  • The cost of renting a car

CAR RENTAL RATE IN BAKU DOES NOT INCLUDE:

  • Fuel cost
  • Washing cost
  • Parking cost
  • Payment of fines for traffic violations
  • Internal and external damages, as well as tire damage
